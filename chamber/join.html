Membership Levels

Bronze Level

Bronze


With the Bronze level, businesses have access to networking events and workshops, as well as being listed in the Chamber of Commerce directory. This level is ideal for small businesses that are just starting and want to increase their visibility in the local community. Additionally, Bronze members receive monthly newsletters with updates on events and business opportunities in the region.

Silver Level

Silver


The Silver level includes all the benefits of Bronze, plus featured advertising at events and on the Chamber's website, and access to specialized consulting services. Silver businesses also have the opportunity to participate in local fairs and exhibitions with exclusive discounts. This level is perfect for businesses looking to expand their market presence and connect with other professionals in the area.

Gold Level

Gold


The Gold level offers all the benefits of the previous levels, plus exclusive sponsorship opportunities, participation in leadership committees, and greater visibility in the city's marketing campaigns. Gold members also have priority access to high-level events and can influence important decisions within the Chamber of Commerce. This level is ideal for established businesses seeking a leadership role in the Mogi Mirim business community.
